Instructions

HOW TO USE CUMULATIVE QPI CALCULATOR

STEP 1: Copy grades from AISIS website.
STEP 2: Click the FILL button and paste your grades in the pop-up text field. Click FILL UP to automatically add your grades into the app.
STEP 3: Enter your DESIRED CUMULATIVE QPI in the provided text field
STEP 4: Enter the number of units you have this current semester
STEP 5: View MINIMUM REQUIRED QPI to see what QPI you need to get for the current semester to reach your TARGET END QPI

NOTE: Subjects with an NSTP, INTAC, or PE course code is automatically excluded when filling up your grades
